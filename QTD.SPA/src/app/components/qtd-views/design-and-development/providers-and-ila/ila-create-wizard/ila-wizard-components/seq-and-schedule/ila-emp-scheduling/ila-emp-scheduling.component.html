If you are ready to schedule classes and enroll student for this {{("ILA" | labelReplacement)| async}}, click the Open Schedules/Roster to open the Scheduling window.

The {{("ILA" | labelReplacement)| async}} will remain in Draft/Development mode until at least one class has been scheduled. After one class has been scheduled, and enrollment options selected, the {{("ILA" | labelReplacement)| async}} will change to a Published status. At that time, all changes to this {{("ILA" | labelReplacement)| async}} will be tracked as version updates.
